# OpenClaw

**Description:** A personal AI assistant that connects to users across dozens of channels (WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, SMS, etc.).
**Key Features:**
- **Multi-channel Inbox:** Single gateway for managing multiple messaging platforms.
- **Live Canvas:** Agent-driven visual workspace.
- **Voice Wake:** Push-to-talk and continuous voice capabilities.
- **Agent Workspace:** Custom skills, tools, and session-specific states.
